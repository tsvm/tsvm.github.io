Tsvetomila Mihaylova

Instituto de Telecomunicações, Lisbon, Portugal

I am a PhD student in the DeepSPIN project at Instituto de Telecomunicações in Lisbon, Portugal, supervised by André Martins and Vlad Niculae. My work is on Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing, with my current research focused on models with latent structures.

Research Interests

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Machine Learning
